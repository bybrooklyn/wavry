# Hosted Service Terms (Minimal)

Wavry operates an official hosted service that provides:
- authentication
- matchmaking
- fallback relay

## Usage Policy
- **Personal / non-commercial use:** free and unlimited.
- **Commercial use:** requires a commercial agreement.
- **Self-hosting:** always allowed under AGPL.

## Relay Limits (Official Service)
- Official relay is **fallback only** (P2P is preferred).
- Sessions are capped at **1080p / 60fps / ~10 Mbps**.

## Community Relays
- Community relays are allowed.
- Relays must register with auth.
- Relays only forward traffic (no identity minting).
- All sessions originate from auth.

## Disclaimer
- Service is provided **as-is**.
- Abuse or excessive misuse may result in service denial.
